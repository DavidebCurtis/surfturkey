Our Story

Get to know who we are and why we do what we do.

Greetings! My name is Trey and I am a web developer. I am learning the skills to develop amazing web projects! I decided to startup my own web development firm while finishing my associates degree in web technologies at Forsyth Technical Community College. I am a native of Asheboro, NC and I am currently residing in Winston Salem, NC. I currently work in tech support role in the healthcare industry here in Winston. I live with my fiance, Courtney, and we've been together since 2019.

I enjoy a morning pot of coffee and time with my two adopted dogs, Bodhi and Woods. I also enjoy games of all sorts. Party games, strategy games (my favorite is backgammon), card games, and the occasional video game although I definitely don't consider myself a 'gamer', unlike most tech-heads. I find pleasure in music of all kinds and I love going to see live music events. I mostly listen to many sub-genres of rock, reggae-rock (a fusion genre also known as 'good vibes'), and metal. I recently have been turned on to lo-fi. It provides a deeply satisfying auditory experience if you haven't listened to it before; I highly recommend it.

I found my love for web development all the way back in 1997 and even developed a few websites for companies in the time before Y2K. My path moved away from tech and into the service industry after high school, and it wasn't until 2019 that I really stepped back and realized that I didn't particularly enjoy working in that industry anymore. I felt it was time for a change. I reflected on what brought me joy since I was a young man, and that is the Internet and the complexities of how it works. So I took an entry-level job in the tech field and I set out to develop web content in my spare time. This move inspired me to finish my associate degree in web technologies and I will graduate in May 2024. It was during this period of time that I met my good friend Adia Chue and inspired her to follow me into the world of web design. Together we make up the foundation of SurfTurkey.net, and you can read more about her below.

My exposure to web technologies has been vast thanks to the curriculum at FTCC. I have greatly extended my knowledge of HTML and CSS, and I'm quickly becoming familiar with the JavaScript language - the three together are the essential building-blocks of all web content on the Internet today. I am also becoming exposed to server-side languages like PHP and Python, and database management skills using SQL and MongoDB.
